Banana Pancakes

Description

Have a tasty breakfast with these banana pancakes. This is an easy recipe that can be put together in 15 minutes. Only bananas and other simple kitchen supplies are needed.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder in one bowl
  2. Combine egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas in another bowl
  3. Add first bowl into second bowl and mix.
  4. Pour batter in 1/4 cup portions onto oiled pan over medium-high heat for few minutes
  5. Flip with spatula and cook for a few minutes
  6. Serve and enjoy!
